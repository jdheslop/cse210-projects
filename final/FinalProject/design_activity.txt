Jackson Heslop

Open-Ended Project

The Overview class tracks the my To Do Items, the meetings, and people.
The Item classes contains the information and status of the to do items and information items.
The Category classes contain the People and Meetings that I can assign items to.
The Menu classes display the various menus needed to display or gather in the information.
